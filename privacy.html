Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 8, 2026

Riverlight Labs ("we," "our," or "us") builds and operates mobile applications and related digital tools, including Lucidra, Plot Twist, and ReadyBinder. This Privacy Policy explains how information may be collected, used, shared, retained, and protected when using our products and services.

Information we may collect

  • Contact information you provide when reaching out for support
  • Account identifiers and sign-in information used to operate app services
  • App usage, diagnostics, and crash information
  • Content you voluntarily provide within the app, such as journal entries or settings
  • Subscription status and purchase entitlement information
  • Profile details you choose to provide, such as display name, avatar, Circle membership, and gameplay activity
  • Device tokens used to send notifications when you opt in

How information may be used

  • To operate, maintain, and improve the app
  • To respond to support requests
  • To analyze app stability and performance
  • To provide requested features and personalized functionality
  • To confirm subscription access and prevent misuse of free usage limits
  • To sync purchases, restore access, run private Circles, assign daily prompts, and deliver requested notifications

Lucidra journal and AI features

Lucidra may store dream journal content, dream signs, training history, settings, and progress data on your device and, when app services require it, through our backend services. If you use AI coaching, the message you send and relevant app context may be processed to generate a response.

We recommend avoiding sensitive personal, medical, financial, or identifying information in dream journal entries or AI coach messages.

Plot Twist gameplay and social features

Plot Twist stores the information needed to run private Circles, assign daily bits, resolve accusations, reveal results, track scores, restore purchased bit packs, and deliver notifications you enable. The app does not use the microphone to listen to real-world play and does not record audio or video for gameplay detection.

If you choose a custom profile photo, the image may be checked for basic safety moderation before it is saved as your avatar. Rejected images are not stored as profile avatars.

Third-party services

Our apps may use third-party services including Firebase and Supabase for authentication, infrastructure, backend services, diagnostics, and app data; RevenueCat for purchase and entitlement management; Apple for App Store purchases, Sign in with Apple, and device services; Google for sign-in and avatar safety checks; Expo for push notification delivery; and OpenAI for Lucidra AI-related functionality. These providers may process information according to their own privacy policies and are expected to protect user data consistently with this policy and applicable requirements.

Data retention

We retain information only as long as reasonably necessary to operate the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, maintain purchase records, and support user-requested features. You can contact us to request deletion of account-related data. Some purchase, transaction, security, or legal records may need to be retained where required by law or platform rules.

Your choices

You can contact us to ask privacy questions, request deletion of account-related data, revoke optional consent where applicable, or ask for help with data associated with your account. Some information may also be managed directly on your device or through your Apple account, depending on the feature. You can turn off push notifications in your device settings.
