Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 1, 2026

Riverlight Labs (“we,” “our,” or “us”) builds and operates mobile applications and related digital tools, including Lucidra. This Privacy Policy explains how information may be collected, used, and protected when using our products and services.

Information we may collect

  • Contact information you provide when reaching out for support
  • Anonymous account identifiers used to operate app services
  • App usage, diagnostics, and crash information
  • Content you voluntarily provide within the app, such as journal entries or settings
  • Subscription status and purchase entitlement information

How information may be used

  • To operate, maintain, and improve the app
  • To respond to support requests
  • To analyze app stability and performance
  • To provide requested features and personalized functionality
  • To confirm subscription access and prevent misuse of free usage limits

Lucidra journal and AI features

Lucidra may store dream journal content, dream signs, training history, settings, and progress data on your device and, when app services require it, through our backend services. If you use AI coaching, the message you send and relevant app context may be processed to generate a response.

We recommend avoiding sensitive personal, medical, financial, or identifying information in dream journal entries or AI coach messages.

Third-party services

Our apps may use third-party services including Firebase for authentication, infrastructure, backend services, diagnostics, and app data; RevenueCat for subscription entitlement management; Apple for App Store purchases and device services; and OpenAI for AI-related functionality. These providers may process information according to their own privacy policies.

Data retention

We retain information only as long as reasonably necessary to operate the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

Your choices

You can contact us to ask privacy questions or request help with account-related data. Some information may also be managed directly on your device or through your Apple account, depending on the feature.
