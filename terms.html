IEP Prep App

ReadyBinder is an organizational and educational tool that helps parents prepare for, take notes during, and follow up on Individualized Education Program (IEP) meetings. It uses AI to read IEP documents you import and to generate plain-English briefs, suggested questions, change summaries, debriefs, follow-up email drafts, and assistant chat responses.
