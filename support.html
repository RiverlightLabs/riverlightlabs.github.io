Subscriptions and billing

Lucid Pro subscriptions are purchased through Apple's App Store. Subscription management, cancellation, billing questions, and refund requests are handled through your Apple account.

If you purchased Lucid Pro and do not see access in the app, use Restore Purchases from the subscription screen or contact support.
