For help with sign-in, Circle invites, purchases, avatar uploads, push notifications, or data requests, email support with the account email or phone number you use in the app.

Purchases and billing

Lucid Pro subscriptions are purchased through Apple's App Store. Subscription management, cancellation, billing questions, and refund requests are handled through your Apple account.

If you purchased Lucid Pro and do not see access in the app, use Restore Purchases from the subscription screen or contact support.

Plot Twist bit packs are one-time App Store purchases. If a purchased pack does not appear, use Restore Purchases in the app or contact support.
