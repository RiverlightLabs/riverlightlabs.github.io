Purchases & Billing

Purchase not showing up?

All purchases go through Apple. Look for a Restore Purchases option on the subscription or store screen inside the app.

Subscriptions and refunds are managed through your Apple ID at Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions. If something still looks wrong, email us.

Sign-in & Access

Can't sign in or lost access?

For Plot Twist, include the name of your Circle if you're having trouble with group access.
